Social Media Awareness and Usage

Objectives

  • Understand what social media is and its different platforms.
  • Learn how to create and manage a social media account.
  • Explore the benefits and risks of social media.
  • Recognize the importance of responsible social media usage.

What is Social Media?

Social media refers to websites and applications that enable users to create and share content or participate in social networking. It encompasses platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and WhatsApp.

Creating and Managing a Social Media Account

  • Choose a platform that aligns with your interests (e.g., Instagram for photos, Twitter for short messages).
  • Sign up on the platform's website or download the app.
  • Fill out your profile information, including your name and profile picture.
  • Adjust privacy settings to control who can see your posts and profile information.

Responsible Social Media Usage

To use social media responsibly, consider the following tips:

  • Think before you post; consider the impact of your words and images.
  • Be respectful and kind to others online.
  • Protect your personal information by being cautious about what you share.
  • Report any harmful or abusive content you encounter.
